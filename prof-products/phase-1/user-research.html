A literature study was conducted in order to gain some level of understanding and context about the topics and sectors related to the assignment:


  1. In-store Operations

    Research on in-store operations and the responsibilities of restaurant owners, and associates within the foodservice industry.

  2. QR Ordering System

    Research on the benefits of QR ordering system and its standardized functionalities in different use cases.

  3. CRM & SaaS & OOS

    Research on the concepts of Customer Relations Management, Software as a Service, and Online Ordering System, and its real-life applications on different use cases.

  4. Data Visualization

    Research on data visualization and different types of methods used to represent data.
