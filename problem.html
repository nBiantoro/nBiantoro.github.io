Problem Statement

The Platform

Obero’s leading solution is a subscriber-based management platform used by restaurateurs to assist in oversight and managing their daily operations. This platform has features to help Obero communicate and assist restaurateurs in managing their food service operations, including a module for creating digital menus, ordering systems and customer care.


However, the current platform could be more user-friendly; it requires high technical knowledge for a person to operate, leading to slow troubleshooting in-between subscribers. Hence to solve that issue, Obero is shifting focus towards a partner-relationship model, where intermediaries (hereafter, vendors) can offer an online food ordering system through Software as a Service (SaaS). With this new relationship model, vendors can offer Value Added Services (VAS) under their brand, incorporating Obero’s management platform with functionalities that offer a state-of-the-art online ordering system and using it to manage their affiliated restaurateurs without requiring huge investments.




The App

Apart from the vendors and restaurateurs, Obero aims to improve the in-store experience for customers, mainly by conducting field research on current pain points and opportunities within food service locations. This observation will help implement the redesign of the platform and the online ordering application to support the in-store experience. Obero wants to provide a comprehensive solution combining the best of online and in-store experiences for customers so that vendors can offer the best values from their services.
